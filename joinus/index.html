Join us

Help RepMat growing with your structures and samples.

RepMat is a collaborative distributed materials library. Different physical structures can be showcased through the virtual part of the library, creating a single database and a virtual network. The library itself can grow thanks to the contribution and collaboration of different users and local communities.

If you are replicating or creating your own physical structures and sets of samples, contact us for being added to the database of the virtual library and be included as a contributor!

Click on the following buttons, write us a message, and you will be contacted as soon as possible. We will send you all the information that we need to add your work in the RepMat library. You can also contact us in case of modifications and further versions of the physical structures or replication materials to be featured in the RepMat website.



Contact us!


You can contact us for more information or contribution requests by clicking on the following buttons and writing an email or a private message (LinkedIn, Instagram, or ResearchGate). You can also contact us for other inquiries and information by using the same contacts.


We will reply to your message as soon as possible! Thanks for your interest in this project!:)
